JavaScript Operators Example

Equality Operators:

== (equal to): 5 == '5' is

=== (equal value and equal type): 5 === '5' is

!= (not equal): 5 != 10 is

!== (not equal value or not equal type): 5 !== '5' is

Comparison Operators:

> (greater than): 10 > 5 is

< (less than): 5 < 10 is

>= (greater than or equal to): 5 >= 5 is

<= (less than or equal to): 10 <= 5 is

Ternary Operator:

Example: age < 18 ? 'Minor' : 'Adult' is
